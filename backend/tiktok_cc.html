    Non-Spark ads

    Spark Ads is an ad format that enables you to leverage native TikTok content, including posts from your account or organic posts made by other creators (with their authorization).

    Non-spark ads are regular in-feed ads. Unlike spark ads, non-spark ads do not attribute views, comments, shares, likes, and follows gained from boosting the video during the promotion to your organic posts.
